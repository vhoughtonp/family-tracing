Search for your family online

The British Red Cross has a database called Trace the Face. It has the the names and faces of people across the world who are looking for their families.

This service is best for people who:

  • don't know which country to look in
  • or have safety concerns.
  • are over 18

Registration is easy, you will need:

  • Your name and contact details
  • The name of your missing relative
  • A photo of your face

We will check for your consent, and send you confirmation email that you have started your trace. You can use this to support an asylum claim.
