You are eligible for an Offline Search

Based on your answers, we advise you search for your family offline. We add to a secure database of people across the world searching for their family.

Your relative can find your details at any of the global Red Cross centres near them.

Why this service is right for you

An offline tracing service is right for people who are:

  • under 18
  • not sure where to start a search
  • in hiding or at risk.

How to get started

Complete a short registration form. You will need:

  • Your name and contact details
  • The name of your missing relative

What happens once you've registered

Once you complete the registration form:

  • You will receive email confirmation
  • You can start looking for your family online
  • We will add your details to the Red Cross international database so that your family can find you.

You are eligible for an Online Search

Based on your answers, we advise you register for our online family tracing service, called Trace the Face.

Why this service is right for you

An online tracing service is right for people who:

  • are over 18
  • don't know which country to look in
  • are in hiding or would be at risk.

How to register

Complete a short online registration form. You will need:

  • Your name and contact details
  • The name of your missing relative
  • A photo of your face (optional)

What happens once you've registered

Once you complete the online registration form:

  • You will receive email confirmation of your enquiry
  • You can start looking for your family online
  • We will create your online profile, so that your missing relative can start looking for you.

You are eligible for an Active Search

Based on your answers, we advise you register for an active trace.

We use a combination of online tools and international volunteers to find your family.

Why this service is right for you

An active tracing service is right for people who:

  • know where to start the search
  • aren't in hiding.

How to get started

Complete a short online enquiry form. You will need:

  • Your name and contact details.
  • The name and possible location of your missing relative

What happens once you've registered

Once you complete the online enquiry form:

  • You will receive email confirmation of your enquiry
  • You can start looking for your family online
  • We will contact you to discuss next steps and book an appointment.
