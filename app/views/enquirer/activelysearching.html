1. Actively searching

If you have enough information about your relatives, we can go out and look for them in person.

Useful things to know:

  • It's public: with your permission, we share your info with people who can help us find your family.
  • It takes time: searches can take months, and often take years.
  • It's thorough: we visit villages and camps, send messages and check records.

Who is this service right for?

  • Feel safe if we publicise the search, ask questions and check public records.
  • Have enough information about their relatives and where they might be.
  • Are looking in a country that is currently accepting enquiries.
