Finding missing family

We have four services to help find your family:

  1. In person: in villages, towns, cities or camps
  2. Online search: create a profile or search for family on Trace the face
  3. Red Cross databases: secure database of people looking for family
  4. Public databases: Such as tax, employment, birth and death databases in different countries

You can use some, or all of these services, depending on your circumstances.

Answer this short questionnaire to find out which service is right for you.
