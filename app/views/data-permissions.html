How the Red Cross shares information during a trace

To find your family we need to take information from you about your family, such as their names, ages, and possible location. Depending on your preferences, we then use this information to:

  • • Search for your family online
  • • Check government databases in the UK and abroad
  • • Send uniformed Red Cross volunteers to locations where your family might be

If you lost contact with your family under dangerous circumstances, sharing your information in this way could put you, or your family in danger. We will only offer you services you feel are safe.
